Welcome to College Thrift


Mission Statement

This site is designed by students for students. We wanted to create a place where students could safely buy and sell their unwanted items. All users must register with a valid ithaca.edu email and no personal information other than name will be distributed.


Safety

Even though all users must have a valid ithaca.edu email, there is still a risk factor. Follow these tips to help you stay safe:

  • Do not interact with people with fake names
  • Use caution with users that do not have an image of themselves
  • Make sure someone knows where you are going
  • Do not meet at night
  • Meet in populated areas on campus
  • Report any suspicious activity to campus security
